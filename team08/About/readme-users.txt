In the main menu, pressing Start will start the game, pressing Exit will exit the game.

WASD keys will move around the space.

Use mouse to look around.

Clicking E when near and looking at the candle will equipped it. Pressing E when holding the candle will unequip it.

Pressing ESC will pause the game and open the pause menu.

There are three golden coins placed around the environment, you need to find them to "win" the game.
